Sus clicker remastered

An exicting game about clicking a sussy baka, one cannot find a better game than this one!

Sus clicker remastered, as per the title name, is a remastered version of my first game called "Sus clicker", sus clicker was a little project I worked on during my time at school, using the unity game engine. I would pump out updates every week or so but due to the fact I had only just started coding, my skills were very limited, causing the game to become a terrible mess, this lead to me creating the "big change" will broke the balance of the entire game making it beatable in like 12 minutes, undesirable. So after about 6 months I found out about godot engine and that's when my work begun (after I had already made a few games with godot). The game was supposed to be a little remaster, having some of the shops from the original and all the same features, and it still does, but the game has a lot more new features, such as new shops, new prestige systems and none of the old annoying bugs, numbers in this game can also reach infinity beacause of the godot big numbers addon! This game is my pride and joy and I love it dearly and if you play it, I hope you love it too!
